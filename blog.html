Do Cats Know Their Name?

Many cat owners wonder if their furry friends recognize their names.
While cats may not respond to their names like dogs do, they often show signs of recognition through their behavior.
Understanding how cats communicate can help you better connect with your feline companion.

Tips for Cat Owners

Feed your cat healthy food. Choose suitable food dishes and water bowls.
Brush your cat regularly.
Bath your cat (but not too often).
Keep your cat's claws trimmed.
Enjoy playtime!
